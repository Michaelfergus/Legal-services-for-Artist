Client Intake Form

Conflict Check

We require the following information in advance of your appointment in order for a volunteer lawyer to check for potential conflicts of interest. Any person(s) you provide will not be contacted. Please provide the names of the individuals/organizations/companies that you know are currently involved in or may be related to your legal issue. Give a brief description of how they are connected to your legal issue (e.g. landlords, producers, publishers, subcontractors, collaborators, partners, co-owners, managers and clients) and, if known to you, the name of any lawyer representing them.

If you have sought and obtained other legal assistance for this issue, please provide any details you can, including the name(s) of any lawyer(s) you have consulted about your issue. This is necessary to ensure that a volunteer lawyer will be able to meet with you.
